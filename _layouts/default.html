What?

Hosted by Bloom Publishing and Hillvale, FOCUS is an annual camera and zine fair. Bringing together some of the best camera hoarders and printed matter enthusiasts Australia has to offer.

When & where?

FOCUS will take place on Sunday August 27th, 10 - 4pm in the Abbotsford Convent Community & Linen Rooms — get directions. This year we'll be asking for a gold coin donation on entry.

Who?

This year we've got tables from the likes of:

  • Perimeter Books
  • Analog Cameras
  • Knowledge Editions
